Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2025 · Last updated: April 5, 2025

Morgenruf ("we", "our", "us") operates the Slack application and website at morgenruf.dev. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

When you install Morgenruf in your Slack workspace, we collect:

  • Workspace information — your Slack team ID, team name, and the bot token issued to us by Slack.
  • User information — Slack user IDs, display names, email addresses (if granted), and timezones of members who participate in standups.
  • Standup responses — the text of standup submissions (yesterday, today, blockers).
  • Configuration — your chosen standup schedule, channel, and question settings.

We do not collect or read any messages outside of direct messages sent to the Morgenruf bot, or public channel messages where the bot is explicitly mentioned.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To send standup prompts to team members at scheduled times.
  • To post standup summaries to your configured channel.
  • To generate per-project and team reports.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., installation confirmation) via Resend.

We do not sell your data. We do not use standup content for advertising or AI training.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway.app infrastructure in the United States. Bot tokens are encrypted at rest. Standup data is retained for 90 days by default, after which it is automatically deleted.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following sub-processors:

  • Slack Technologies — to deliver messages via the Slack API.
  • Railway.app — database and application hosting.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Netlify — website hosting.

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all your workspace data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling Morgenruf from your Slack workspace (triggers automatic data deletion within 30 days), or
  • Emailing privacy@morgenruf.dev with your Slack team ID.

6. Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Slack tokens are stored encrypted. We follow Slack's security best practices including token rotation. We conduct periodic security reviews of our infrastructure.

7. Children's Privacy

Morgenruf is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify workspace admins via Slack DM for material changes. Continued use after notice constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@morgenruf.dev or open an issue at github.com/morgenruf/morgenruf.
